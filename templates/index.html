Why PCOD Awareness Matters

Polycystic Ovarian Disease (PCOD) affects millions of women during their reproductive years, yet many remain unaware of their condition due to mild, irregular, or ignored symptoms.

Delayed diagnosis can lead to long-term complications such as hormonal imbalance, fertility issues, insulin resistance, and emotional distress. Early awareness plays a crucial role in managing PCOD effectively.

Our AI-based screening tool helps women identify potential PCOD risk early using symptom-based and lifestyle inputs, without the need for lab reports.

What is PCOD?

Polycystic Ovarian Disease is a hormonal disorder where the ovaries may produce excess androgens, leading to irregular ovulation. This can result in menstrual irregularities, metabolic issues, and visible physical symptoms. PCOD varies from person to person, making early symptom recognition essential.

⚠️

Common Symptoms

Look out for irregular periods, excessive hair growth, acne, weight gain, and hair thinning. Symptoms often appear around puberty.
